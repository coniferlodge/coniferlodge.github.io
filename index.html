Beautiful Retreat

Conifer Lodge is located 3 miles from downtown McCall Idaho. 2 blocks from Ponderosa State Park and Payette Lake. McCall Municipal Golf Course is 3 blocks away.

McCall is located 100 miles north of Boise Idaho. The scenic drive on route 55 runs along the North Fork of the Payette River. Or, if you prefer there are shuttle flights from the Boise Airport to the McCall airport. Most major airlines have multiple daily flights into Boise.
